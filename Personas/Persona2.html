Persona 2

Name: George Moraru

Date of Birth: 22/02/2001

Gender: Male

Location: Iasi, Romania

Work place: Front-End Developer

School: Faculty of Computer Science, UAIC

George is a software developer at the start of his road. He is now in a continuous process of learning and career development, so days at work can be exhausting and difficult for him. His wish for a good afternoon is to find himself a movie to watch and relax.

Main Points

  1. He is passionate about movies
  2. He keeps records on top and movie awards
  3. He loves old movies, but also he tastes very well the new generation of movies

Main Goals

  1. He wants to get recommandations based on his taste
  2. He wants to have alternatives in case of not finding anything properly

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A hard day at work

George had a rough day at work and wants to relax watching a horror movie. He can enter on the app and see the recommandations for his favourite genre.

Scenario 2: Friday movie night

It's friday and George forgot to pick a movie for tonight. He will enter the app and see the top 10 movies to get some quality time on screen.
