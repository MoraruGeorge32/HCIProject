Persona 3

Name: Mihai Nedelcu

Date of Birth: 12/04/1995

Gender: Male

Location: Iasi, Romania

Work place: Student

School: Faculty of Economics, UAIC

Mihai is a student with a lot of spare time at his disposal. He wants to find new movies to binge while the time passes.

Main Points

  1. He is full of free time.
  2. He developed critique opinions based on the loads of movies and tv shows he watched.

Main Goals

  1. He wants to create a list with movies he will watch in the next period
  2. He wants to create a list with movies he watched and rate them

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Wants to add a movie to the list

Mihai wants to add the new Star Wars movie to the list to watch it in the future. He will open the burger menu and access the My List page.

Scenario 2: Wants to see if there are movies in the top he didn't watch

Mihai is a big fan of action movies. He wants to find out if there are movies he didn't watch in top 10 based on IMDB rating and add them to the list.
