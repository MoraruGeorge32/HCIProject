Movie Lounge


On Netflix there are 3781 movies and 2157 shows. On HBO Max there are 2000+ movies and more than 580 TV shows. On Amazon Prime there are more than 26300 movies. Even so, you barely know what to watch in the night after a hard day at work. It's time to change this! Movie Lounge is proposing an application which recommends movies based on your taste. If you want horror, you can specifically choose your favourite kind of horror, like slasher or demonic possession type of movie.


The application opens with the homepage, where you can find out what this application can do for the user and its range(Netflix, HBO Max, Amazon Prime, Apple TV). From there, you can sign up or log in. The sign up process is customizable. After you authenticate on the application, on the first page you will find:

  • Our recommandations for the current week
  • A carousel of upcoming movies which are hot for the public
  • Search bar in order to find certain movies
  • Burger menu with all the features of the application and profile picture
